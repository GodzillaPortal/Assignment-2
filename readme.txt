Javascript Validation:
Recommend logic:
- Budget slider (Detection when slid, updates text)
- Dietary filters (Detection when checked, updates text)
- Occasion filter (Detects when occasion is changed, updates text)
Register Logic:
- Username cannot be empty
- Email cannot be empty and must contain an @ and .
- Phone number must be digits only between 8 - 15 characters
- Password cannot be empty, must be at least 10 characters with an upper cases, lower cases, numbers, and special characters
- Password confirmation must match password
- Gender must be selected
- Country/Region cannot be empty
Reservation Logic:
- Email cannot be empty and must contain an @
- Phone number needs 10 digits
- Reservation date cannot be in the past
- Number of people must be more than 0
- Payment must either be online or voucher
- Card is either 15 or 16 digits
- Voucher has to be 12 digits

Known issues/Limitations:
Unfortunately due to time constraints and my general programming abilities there are some limitations with the site.
- Recommendation page with slider recommending new restaurants causes page to change size, warping background with it.
- Mobile view is not perfect
- Not all validation errors might trigger depending on specific circumstances.
- Submit buttons on some pages are small due to validator tool outlining that changing text sizes within buttons is illegal.
